  1. Achieve *zero-landfill* status for all non-hazardous commercial waste streams by 2028.

Know Your Plastics: Quick Guide

Ensure these items are empty and rinsed before placing them in the blue bin:

  • *PET (#1):* Clear soda bottles, water bottles.
  • *HDPE (#2):* Milk jugs, shampoo containers, detergent bottles.
  • *Mixed Paper:* Junk mail, newspapers, magazines (no shredded paper).
